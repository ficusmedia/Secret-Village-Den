Property Information

Situated in Sarandë, just 2.7 km from Saranda City Beach, Secret Village Den features beachfront accommodation with a bar, a shared lounge, a garden and free WiFi. This apartment offers free private parking and a 24-hour front desk.

The apartment consists of 1 separate bedroom, 1 bathroom, and a seating area. For added convenience, the property can provide towels and bed linen for an extra charge.

A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the apartment. (3€)

Secret Village Den - Camping offers a barbecue. A car rental service is available at the accommodation.
